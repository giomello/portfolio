About Me

Hello, my name is Gio

MARKETING SPECIALIST
DIGITAL & GRAPHIC DESIGNER

I’m a designer at heart and a marketer by trade, blending creativity with strategy to build brands that stand out. With a background in graphic and digital design, I’ve worked on everything from branding and illustration to user-friendly websites and engaging social media content.

What excites me most is the intersection of design and marketing, where visuals meet strategy to create campaigns that resonate. I’ve had the chance to collaborate with cross-functional teams, bringing ideas to life through thoughtful storytelling and data-driven marketing. Whether it’s crafting compelling visuals or optimizing digital experiences, I’m always looking for new ways to make an impact.

Outside of work, I enjoy photography, vinyl records, exploring the outdoors, web development, soccer, snowboarding and cycling around the city.
